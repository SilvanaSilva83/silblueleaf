Welcome to our restaurant

The Blue Leaf Restaurant, situated in the heart of Bundoran, has a captivating history. It occupies the site where an old Methodist church once stood—Campbell’s Memorial Methodist Church, built by Thomas Elliott in 1888. In 1980, the church underwent a transformation, becoming a cozy coffee shop. However, since March 2018, new owners have taken the reins, renaming it to The Blue Leaf Restaurant.
The building’s unique character is accentuated by its gothic windows and a glazed porch that faces the bustling street. Over the years, it has become synonymous with high-quality, generous, and tasty food. The carefully curated menu features well-chosen dishes, emphasizing the use of fresh local ingredients—a commitment to supporting Irish producers.
As a small family restaurant, The Blue Leaf prioritizes creating an exceptional gastronomic experience for its patrons. Whether you’re seeking an intimate dinner or celebrating a special occasion, the warm and familiar atmosphere welcomes you. The restaurant is skillfully managed by Ania Gapa-Chochorowska and Chef De Cuisine Grzegorz Chochorowski. Their culinary expertise shines through in every dish, making The Blue Leaf a go-to spot for comfort food in Bundoran12.
If you find yourself strolling along Bundoran’s main street, don’t miss the chance to savor their high-quality offerings!
